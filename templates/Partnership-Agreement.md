# Partnership Agreement

1. <span class="header_2">Cooperation</span>
    1. <span class="header_3">Obligations.</span>  Each party will perform its <span class="businessterms_link">Obligations</span> as detailed in the Cover Page.
    2. <span class="header_3">Feedback.</span>  Each party may, but is not required to, give Feedback to the other party. All Feedback is given "AS IS". The party receiving Feedback may use it freely without any restriction or obligation.
2. <span class="header_2">Payment & Taxes</span>
    1. If the <span class="businessterms_link">Obligations</span> include payment of Fees, the following terms apply:
        a. <span class="header_3">Fees and Billing.</span>  Unless the Cover Page specifies a different currency, all Fees are in U.S. Dollars and are exclusive of taxes. Fees are non-refundable except for the prorated refund of prepaid Fees.
        b. <span class="header_3">Payment.</span>  The paying party will pay applicable Fees and related taxes according to the <span class="businessterms_link">Payment Schedule</span>.
        c. <span class="header_3">Taxes.</span>  The paying party is responsible for all duties, taxes, and levies that apply to Fees.
3. <span class="header_2">Trademark License</span>
    1. <span class="header_3">Trademark License.</span>  Licensor grants Licensee a non-exclusive, non-transferrable, revocable, royalty-free license in the <span class="businessterms_link">Territory</span> to use Licensor's Brand Elements solely as necessary for Licensee to perform its <span class="businessterms_link">Obligations</span>.
    2. <span class="header_3">Reservation of Rights.</span>  Licensor is the sole and exclusive owner of all right, title, and interest in and to the Brand Elements.
    3. <span class="header_3">Restrictions on Licensee.</span>  Licensee will not alter, combine, or use the Brand Elements in a way that implies endorsement beyond the scope of the Agreement or that might harm Licensor's reputation.
4. <span class="header_2">Privacy</span>
    1. If the parties have a <span class="keyterms_link">DPA</span>, each party will comply with its obligations in the <span class="keyterms_link">DPA</span>.
5. <span class="header_2">Escalation Procedure</span>
    1. Each party agrees to give the other written notice of disputes prior to seeking legal relief. Within 30 days, at least one representative from each party will hold a meeting attempting to resolve the dispute in good faith.
6. <span class="header_2">Term & Termination</span>
    1. <span class="header_3">Term.</span>  This Agreement starts on the <span class="keyterms_link">Effective Date</span> and continues until the <span class="businessterms_link">End Date</span>.
    2. <span class="header_3">Termination.</span>  Either party may terminate immediately for material breach (30 days cure), or upon notice for insolvency or dissolution.
    3. <span class="header_3">Effect of Termination.</span>  All trademark license rights immediately terminate. Each Recipient will return or destroy Confidential Information. Outstanding Fees are settled.
    4. <span class="header_3">Survival.</span>  Payment terms, IP reservation, confidentiality, and general terms survive termination.
7. <span class="header_2">Representations & Warranties</span>
    1. Each party represents that it has the legal power to enter into this Agreement and will comply with all Applicable Laws.
8. <span class="header_2">Disclaimer of Warranties</span>
    1. Except for express warranties, both parties disclaim all other warranties, whether express or implied.
9. <span class="header_2">Limitation of Liability</span>
    1. <span class="header_3">Liability Caps.</span>  **Each party's total cumulative liability will not be more than the <span class="keyterms_link">General Cap Amount</span> or <span class="keyterms_link">Increased Cap Amount</span> for Increased Claims.**
    2. <span class="header_3">Damages Waiver.</span>  Neither party will be liable for lost profits or indirect damages.
10. <span class="header_2">Indemnification</span>
    1. Each party will indemnify the other from Covered Claims arising from that party's breach or acts.
11. <span class="header_2">Confidentiality</span>
    1. Recipient will not use or disclose Discloser's Confidential Information except as needed to fulfill obligations under this Agreement.
12. <span class="header_2">General Terms</span>
    1. This Agreement is the entire agreement between the parties. Modifications must be in writing. Governing law and chosen courts are specified in the Cover Page. Independent contractors, no third-party beneficiaries.
13. <span class="header_2">Definitions</span>
    1. **"Standard Terms"** means these Common Paper Partnership Standard Terms Version 1.0, posted at [https://commonpaper.com/standards/partnership-agreement/1.0](commonpaper.com/standards/partnership-agreement/1.0).
    2. **"Brand Elements"** means a party's trademarks, service marks, names, and logos.
    3. **"Confidential Information"** means information identified as confidential or that should be reasonably understood as confidential due to its nature.
    4. **"Feedback"** means suggestions, feedback, or comments about the other party's products or services.
    5. **"Variable"** means a word or phrase in the Standard Terms that is highlighted and capitalized.
