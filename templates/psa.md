# Professional Services Agreement

1. <span class="header_2">Services</span>
    1. <span class="header_3">Providing Services.</span>  <span class="keyterms_link">Customer</span> or its Affiliates may enter SOWs with <span class="keyterms_link">Provider</span>. <span class="keyterms_link">Provider</span> will perform the Services as detailed in an applicable SOW.
    2. <span class="header_3">Cooperation.</span>  <span class="keyterms_link">Customer</span> will reasonably cooperate with <span class="keyterms_link">Provider</span> to allow the performance of Services.
    3. <span class="header_3">Change Orders.</span>  Either party may request a Change Order; it is not binding until both parties agree in writing.
    4. <span class="header_3">Acceptance.</span>  <span class="keyterms_link">Customer</span> will be deemed to have approved a <span class="sow_link">Deliverable</span> if it does not reject the Deliverable within the <span class="sow_link">Rejection Period</span>.
    5. <span class="header_3">Subcontractors.</span>  <span class="keyterms_link">Provider</span> may use Subcontractors only with <span class="keyterms_link">Customer's</span> prior permission, but may use its Affiliates without permission.
    6. <span class="header_3">Customer Obligations.</span>  <span class="keyterms_link">Customer</span> will comply with <span class="sow_link">Customer Obligations</span>, if any.
2. <span class="header_2">Intellectual Property</span>
    1. <span class="header_3">Deliverables.</span>  Except for Pre-Existing Materials and Third-Party Materials, <span class="keyterms_link">Provider</span> assigns all right, title, and interest in the <span class="sow_link">Deliverables</span> to <span class="keyterms_link">Customer</span> at the <span class="sow_link">Time of Assignment</span>.
    2. <span class="header_3">Customer Materials.</span>  <span class="keyterms_link">Provider</span> may use Customer Materials only as needed to provide the Services.
    3. <span class="header_3">Pre-Existing Materials.</span>  <span class="keyterms_link">Provider</span> grants <span class="keyterms_link">Customer</span> a non-exclusive, perpetual license to use Pre-Existing Materials only as necessary to use the Deliverables.
    4. <span class="header_3">Third-Party Materials.</span>  <span class="keyterms_link">Provider</span> may incorporate Third-Party Materials only if allowed in the SOW. <span class="keyterms_link">Provider</span> is responsible for obtaining all rights to Third-Party Materials it procures.
    5. <span class="header_3">Feedback and Usage Data.</span>  <span class="keyterms_link">Provider</span> may use Feedback and Usage Data to maintain, improve, and enhance its products and services.
    6. <span class="header_3">Reservation of Rights.</span>  Neither party transfers any rights in products, data, or intellectual property except as expressly stated.
3. <span class="header_2">Privacy & Security</span>
    1. If the parties have a <span class="keyterms_link">DPA</span>, each party will comply with its obligations in the <span class="keyterms_link">DPA</span>.
    2. <span class="keyterms_link">Provider</span> will comply with the <span class="keyterms_link">Security Policy</span>, if any.
4. <span class="header_2">Payment & Taxes</span>
    1. <span class="header_3">Fees and Invoices.</span>  Unless otherwise specified, all <span class="sow_link">Fees</span> are in U.S. Dollars and exclusive of taxes. Fees are non-refundable except for prorated refunds on specific termination.
    2. <span class="header_3">Payment.</span>  <span class="keyterms_link">Customer</span> will pay Fees and taxes within the <span class="sow_link">Payment Period</span>.
    3. <span class="header_3">Taxes.</span>  <span class="keyterms_link">Customer</span> is responsible for all duties, taxes, and levies that apply to Fees.
5. <span class="header_2">Term & Termination</span>
    1. <span class="header_3">Term.</span>  This Agreement will start on the <span class="keyterms_link">Effective Date</span> and continue until 12 months after the end of the latest <span class="keyterms_link">SOW Term</span>.
    2. <span class="header_3">Termination.</span>  Either party may terminate for uncured material breach, insolvency, Force Majeure, or if there are no active SOWs.
    3. <span class="header_3">Effect of Termination.</span>  Services stop, Confidential Information is returned/destroyed, outstanding Fees are settled.
    4. <span class="header_3">Survival.</span>  IP, payment, confidentiality, indemnification, and general terms survive termination.
6. <span class="header_2">Representations & Warranties</span>
    1. Each party represents it has legal power to enter this Agreement.
    2. <span class="keyterms_link">Provider</span> warrants it will perform Services in a timely, competent, and professional manner and that Deliverables will conform to SOW requirements.
7. <span class="header_2">Disclaimer of Warranties</span>
    1. Except for express warranties, both parties **disclaim all other warranties, whether express or implied.**
8. <span class="header_2">Limitation of Liability</span>
    1. **Each party's total cumulative liability will not be more than the <span class="keyterms_link">General Cap Amount</span> or <span class="keyterms_link">Increased Cap Amount</span> for Increased Claims.**
    2. **Neither party will be liable for lost profits, revenues, or indirect damages.**
9. <span class="header_2">Indemnification</span>
    1. Each party will indemnify the other from Covered Claims arising from that party's breach or acts.
10. <span class="header_2">Insurance</span>
    1. During the term and for six months after, each party will carry commercial insurance policies meeting the relevant <span class="keyterms_link">Insurance Minimums</span>.
11. <span class="header_2">Confidentiality</span>
    1. Recipient will not use or disclose Discloser's Confidential Information except as needed to fulfill obligations under this Agreement.
12. <span class="header_2">General Terms</span>
    1. Entire agreement. Modifications must be in writing. Governing law and chosen courts are specified in the Key Terms. Independent contractors, no third-party beneficiaries, force majeure, export controls, anti-bribery.
13. <span class="header_2">Definitions</span>
    1. **"Agreement"** means these Standard Terms, the Key Terms, and referenced documents.
    2. **"Change Order"** means a document identifying the SOW being changed, approved by both parties.
    3. **"Pre-Existing Materials"** means information, tools, or IP that <span class="keyterms_link">Provider</span> developed or owned before the Effective Date.
    4. **"Services"** means the services described in a SOW, including creation of Deliverables.
    5. **"SOW"** means a Cover Page including the key business details for this Agreement.
    6. **"Subcontractors"** means other people or companies engaged by <span class="keyterms_link">Provider</span> to perform Services.
